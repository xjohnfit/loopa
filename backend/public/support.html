Common questions

How do I delete my account?

Email us from the address on your account and we'll delete your account and all associated data within 7 days. See the Privacy Policy for details.

What's the difference between a recurring and a one-off task?

Recurring tasks are your daily routine — they show up every day until you archive them. One-off tasks are scheduled for a single date and are only visible (and only deletable) on that day.

Why do all tasks need a category?

Categories are how Loopa groups your day so it reads at a glance instead of as a flat list. Every task belongs to exactly one category — create one with a name and color in a few seconds from the task form.

Is my data private?

Yes. Every account is isolated — your tasks and categories are only ever visible to you. See the Privacy Policy for full details.

Is there an Android version?

Not yet — Loopa is currently iOS-only. Android support is planned.
